🎓 Learning Objectives
By using this tool, you will explore:

The Power of Time: How starting at age 22 versus age 35 affects your final nest egg.

Employer Matching: Why a 401k match is often called "free money."

Compound Interest: Seeing how your money grows exponentially over 30+ years.

Inflation Impact: Understanding the difference between "raw" dollars and "inflation-adjusted" purchasing power.

🛠 Features
Interactive Sliders: Adjust your salary, contribution percentage, and expected rate of return.

Visual Graphs: See your balance grow year-by-year through a dynamic chart.

Real-time Calculations: Instantly see how small changes in your contribution can lead to hundreds of thousands of dollars in difference by retirement.
